Word Guess Game

Can you guess the secret word one letter at a time?

📖 How to Play

  1. There is a secret word hidden as blank boxes, like this: _ _ _ _
  2. Pick letters one at a time. Click a letter on the keyboard, or type it.
  3. If the letter is in the word, it fills in the blank. 🎉
  4. If the letter is not in the word, you lose one ❤️ heart.
  5. You start with 6 hearts. Guess the whole word before they run out!
  6. Fill in every letter to win. Run out of hearts and you lose — but you'll see the answer.
